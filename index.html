About Us


We are a mobile disco playing all types of soul music from the 60's ,70's, 80's and also to up to date modern soul, on 7 inch and 12 inch vinyl,specialising in Tamla Motown, Philadelphia, popular Northern Soul and funky classic crossover disco sounds, backed up by an extensive library of CD's and digital tracks to suit your event.

Please see our MUSIC and VIDEO pages to sample a few of our favorite tracks.

We play at various venues, including private parties clubs and the occasional public house, we are available for residencies and regular events providing a classic old school theme, transporting your private party or clientele back to a time when dance music was great.

Our dual Technics turntables are considered a great feature and make a great focal point at any venue.
