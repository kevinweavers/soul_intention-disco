About Soul Intention Disco

We are a mobile disco playing all types of soul music from the 60's ,70's and 80's on 7 inch and 12" vinyl, specialising in Tamla Motown, Philadelphia, popular Northern Soul and funky classic crossover disco sounds. This is backed up by an extensive library of CD's and digital tracks to suit your event.
Please see our MUSIC and VIDEO pages to sample a few of our favorite tracks.

We play a various venues, including private parties and pubs and clubs, we are available for residencies and regular events providing a classic old school theme, transporting your private party or clientele back to a time when dance music was great. Our dual Technics turntables are considered a great feature and make a great focal point at any venue. Enquire about booking us HERE
